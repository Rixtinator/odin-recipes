Recipe for delicious Rugelachs

I didn't know these delicious 'things' excisted untill I saw them in the book from Ottolenghi. These cookies look like croissants but taste much sweeter because of the filling. This recipe is only for the dough, the filling can differ (make it your own!)

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoon lemon zest
  • 1/4 vanille stick
  • 125 gr butter
  • 125 gr creamcheese

Steps

  1. Mix together the flour, salt, baking powder, lemon zest en vanille.
  2. Add the butter and the creamcheese and mix until the dough forms a big ball.
  3. Split the dough in two even balls and put them away in the frigde (with some plastic), for around one hour.
  4. After an hour you van take one of the two balls and roll them out to a circle of 24 cm
  5. Add the filling (get creative).
  6. Cut with a pizzacutter the dough into 12 triangles
  7. Roll the triangles from the outlide to the middle and put them away in the frigde for another 30 minutes.
  8. Add some eggwash and suger and put them in the over at 180 degrees Celsius for about 20 - 25 minutes.
